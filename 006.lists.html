Unordered list to make bullet points
  • One
  • Two
  • Three
    Ordered list to make numbered points
  1. One
  2. Two
  3. Three
If you want to start your ordered list at a particluar number you can write
  1. Ten
  2. Eleven
  3. Twelve
If you want to reverse it you can use
  1. Three
  2. Two
  3. One
You can also specify the type, so
    A is A. B. C a is a, b, c I is big Roman numerals i is small Roman numerals
